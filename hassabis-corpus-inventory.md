## VII. INTELLECTUAL LINEAGE & RECURRING THEMES
*For wiki synthesis use — recurring conceptual spine across the corpus:*

1. **The Construction System** — memory and imagination share a single generative mechanism centred on the hippocampus (N1, N3, N6, T1 → echoed in A9, I3)
2. **Neuroscience as AI roadmap** — the brain solved intelligence; its solutions are the best available inspiration (A9, I7, I3)
3. **Games as tractable AGI testbeds** — Go, StarCraft, chess used as structured environments where progress is measurable (A1, A2, A20, I7)
4. **AI for Science** — AlphaFold as proof-of-concept that AI can solve grand scientific challenges; mathematics, physics, virtual cells next (A24, A28, E3, I3)
5. **Safety as co-equal priority** — consistent argument that AI risk should be addressed before capability arrives, not after (I1, I4, I6)
6. **The "learnable nature" conjecture** — novel claim (Nobel lecture 2024): natural systems contain evolutionary structure that makes them efficiently learnable by classical algorithms (E3, I3)
7. **Responsible commercialisation** — AGI benefits should be redistributed broadly; UBI; international coordination; non-antagonistic US-China dynamics (I1, I4)
